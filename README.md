## About Me

I am passionate about developing AI systems that bridge technology and real-world user needs.

My master's research focused on designing and developing an AI-powered healthcare consultation platform based on Large Language Models (LLMs) and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG). The project was accepted by IEEE COMPSAC 2024 Fast Abstracts and explored the application of conversational AI in Advance Care Planning (ACP) education and consultation.

In addition to healthcare AI, I am interested in AI application development, knowledge retrieval systems, intelligent assistants, and practical AI solutions that create real value for users.
